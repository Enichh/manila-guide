About this landmark

Fort Santiago is a citadel first built in 1571 by Spanish conquistador Miguel López de Legazpi for the new established city of Manila in the Philippines. The fort is part of the defensive walls surrounding the historical city of Intramuros and serves as the site of the Rizal Shrine — dedicated to Philippine national hero José Rizal.
